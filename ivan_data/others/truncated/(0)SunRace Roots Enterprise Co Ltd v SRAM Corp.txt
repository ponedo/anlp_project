Synopsis
Competitor brought declaratory judgment action against patentee, alleging that competitor did not infringe patent directed to bicycle gear-shifting device and that patent was invalid and unenforceable. Patentee counterclaimed for infringement. The United States District Court for the Northern District of California, Vaughn R. Walker, J., granted declaratory judgment of noninfringement, and patentee appealed. The Court of Appeals, Bryson, Circuit Judge, held that term "shift actuator" was not limited to devices that used a cam.
 
Reversed and remanded.
