Synopsis
Background: Assignee of patents for vitamin supplement compositions sued competitor for infringement. The United States District Court for the District of Minnesota, Ann D. Montgomery, J., 2003 WL 22999551 and 2004 WL 902176, found both patents invalid, and assignee appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Rader, Circuit Judge, held that patents were invalid as anticipated.
 
Affirmed.
