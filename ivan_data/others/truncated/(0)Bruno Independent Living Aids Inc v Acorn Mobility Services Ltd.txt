Synopsis
Background: Defendant who prevailed in patent infringement and false advertising suit moved for award of attorney fees. The United States District Court for the Western District of Wisconsin, Barbara B. Crabb, J., 277 F.Supp.2d 965, granted motion, and plaintiff appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Lourie, Circuit Judge, held that plaintiff's failure to disclose material prior art warranted finding that its subsequent infringement action was extraordinary.
 
Affirmed.
