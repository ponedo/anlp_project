DECIDED: Oct. 7, 2003.
Synopsis
Owner of patent for locking mechanism for portable electronic devices sued competitor for infringement. Alleged infringer moved for summary judgment. The United States District Court for the Northern District of California, 2002 WL 1735385, Susan Illston, J., granted summary judgment of noninfringement, and appeal was taken. The Court of Appeals, Pauline Newman, Circuit Judge, held that patent was neither literally nor equivalently infringed.
 
Affirmed
