Synopsis
Patent holder brought action against competitor, alleging infringement of patent for cartoners used to group articles and load them into paperboard cartons. The United States District Court for the Northern District of Georgia, Charles A. Moye, Jr., Senior Judge, held that patent was infringed and was not invalid for obviousness. Competitor appealed. The Court of Appeals, Mayer, Chief Judge, held that patent was invalid for obviousness.
 
Reversed.
