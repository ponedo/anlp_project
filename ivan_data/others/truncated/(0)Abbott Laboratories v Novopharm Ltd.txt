Synopsis
Assignee and licensee of patent for cholesterol drug brought infringement against manufacturer of generic version of drug. The United States District Court for the Northern District of Illinois, John W. Darrah, J., 2002 WL 433584, entered summary judgment in favor of defendant, and plaintiffs appealed. The Court of Appeals, Lourie, Circuit Judge, held that: (1) patent was not literally infringed, and (2) patent was not infringed under doctrine of equivalents.
 
Affirmed.
