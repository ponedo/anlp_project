Synopsis
Background: Patentee brought action against competitor, alleging infringement of patent for sleep-inducing drug. Competitor moved for summary judgment that patent was invalid for obviousness. The United States District Court for the District of New Jersey, Stanley R. Chesler, J., 2010 WL 1799457,granted motion. Patentee appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Bryson, Circuit Judge, held that patent was invalid as obvious.
 
Affirmed.
