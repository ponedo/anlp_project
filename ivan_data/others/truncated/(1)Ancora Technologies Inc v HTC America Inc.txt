Synopsis
Background: Owner of patent describing and claiming methods of limiting a computer's running of software not authorized for that computer to run brought infringement action. Competitor moved to dismiss. The United States District Court for the Western District of Washington, No. 2:16-cv-01919-RAJ, Richard A. Jones, J., 287 F.Supp.3d 1168, granted motion. Patent owner appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Taranto, Circuit Judge, held that claim was not directed to an abstract idea, and thus was not patent ineligible.
 
Reversed and remanded.
 
Procedural Posture(s): On Appeal; Motion to Dismiss for Failure to State a Claim.
