Synopsis
Owner of patentee disclosing "recipe" for producing transgenic mice sued medical research center for infringement. The United States District Court for the Northern District of California, 175 F.Supp.2d 1209, William H. Alsup, J., held that patents were invalid as anticipated, and patentee appealed. Superceding its prior opinion, 243 F.3d 567, 2000 WL 1720837, the Court of Appeals reversed and remanded. On rehearing en banc, the Court of Appeals, Pauline Newman, Circuit Judge, held that prior art reference must be enabling in order to be anticipating.
 
Reversed and remanded.
