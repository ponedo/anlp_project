Synopsis
Background: Owner and licensor filed action against competitor alleging infringement of design patent for tractor tire. The United States District Court for the Southern District of Iowa, James E. Gritzner, J., 2007 WL 2914513,denied plaintiffs' motion for preliminary injunction. Plaintiffs appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Plager, Circuit Judge, held that trial court did not abuse its discretion in concluding that licensor was unlikely to withstand competitor's challenge to validity of patent on obviousness grounds.
 
Affirmed.
