Synopsis
Background: Owner of patent for omni-directional light emitting diode (LED) lamp sued lamp manufacturer and its retail distributors for infringement. The United States District Court for the Western District of Arkansas, Harry F. Barnes, J., granted summary judgment of noninfringement, and owner appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Linn, Circuit Judge, held that patent was not infringed.
 
Affirmed.
