Synopsis
Background: Patentee brought action against competitor, alleging infringement of its patent for an anesthetic sevoflurane composition. The United States District Court for the Northern District of Illinois, 2002 WL 449007, Ronald A. Guzman, J., granted summary judgment for competitor, and patentee appealed. The Court of Appeals, 334 F.3d 1274, vacated and remanded. On remand, the District Court, Guzman, J., 2005 WL 2347221, ruled that the patent was valid but not infringed, and patentee appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Gajarsa, Circuit Judge, held that patent was anticipated by prior art patent.
 
Reversed.
