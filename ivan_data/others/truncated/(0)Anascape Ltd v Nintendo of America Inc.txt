Synopsis
Background: Owner of patent related to hand-operated controllers for the movement of images on a computer screen or television display, particularly as used in video games, brought infringement action. Following jury trial, the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Texas, Ron Clark, J., found infringement, awarded damages, denied duly made post-trial motions, enjoined further infringement, and stayed injunction pending appeal. Defendant appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Newman, Circuit Judge, held that patentee was not entitled to filing date of previously filed application.
 
Reversed.
 
Gajarsa, Circuit Judge, filed concurring opinion.
