Synopsis
Background: Owner of patent relating to a control system providing existing automobile seats with massage functionality brought infringement action against competitors. The United States District Court for the Eastern District of Michigan, Lawrence P. Zatkoff, Senior Judge, 2008 WL 896060,granted summary judgment in favor of competitors. Patent owner appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Lourie, Circuit Judge, held that individual suggesting an extender for a lumbar support adjustor be included in patented invention was not a co-inventor.
 
Reversed and remanded.
