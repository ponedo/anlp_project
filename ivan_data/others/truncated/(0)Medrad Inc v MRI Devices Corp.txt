Synopsis
Background: Owner of patent for overlapping radio frequency coils used in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) sued competitor for infringement. The United States District Court for the Western District of Pennsylvania, Terrence F. McVerry, J., determined that patent was invalid, and owner appealed.
 
The Court of Appeals, Bryson, Circuit Judge, held that patent was invalid as anticipated.
 
Affirmed.
