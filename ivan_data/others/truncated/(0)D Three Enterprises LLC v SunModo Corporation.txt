Synopsis
Background: Owner of patents directed to roof mount sealing assemblies, which allowed users to mount objects on a roof and seal the mounting location against water, brought infringement action against competitors. Parties filed cross-motions for summary judgment. The United States District Court for the District of Colorado, Nos. 1:15-cv-01148-CBS, 1:15-cv-01151-CBS, Craig B. Shaffer, 2017 WL 1023389, United States Magistrate Judge, granted competitors' motion. Patent owner appealed.
 
Holdings: The Court of Appeals, Wallach, Circuit Judge, held that:
 
grant of summary judgment in favor of competitor did not prejudice patent owner, and
 
claims could not claim priority from prior application.
 
Affirmed.
 
Procedural Posture(s): On Appeal; Motion for Summary Judgment.
