Synopsis
Patentee brought suit alleging that defendant made semiconductor devices using process that infringed several claims of plaintiff's patent. The United States District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia, Leonie M. Brinkema, J., ruled that patent was unenforceable due to inequitable conduct. Patentee appealed. The Court of Appeals, Plager, Circuit Judge, held that patentee engaged in inequitable conduct during prosecution of patent application.
 
Affirmed.
